Courses We Offer

Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)

A four-year undergraduate program designed to build strong technical knowledge, practical skills, and industry-ready engineers across multiple disciplines.

Master of Technology (M.Tech)

M.Tech (Master of Technology) is a 2-year postgraduate engineering degree, often in specialized fields like CS, AI, or Structural Engineering, designed to enhance technical expertise for research, R&D roles, and advanced industry positions. Primarily, it leads to careers in technology, teaching, or further doctoral research (PhD).

Bachelor of Vocation (B.Voc)

B.Voc (Bachelor of Vocation) is a 3-year, UGC-approved undergraduate degree focused on skill-based education, industry training, and high employability. It features 60% practical training and 40% theory, often utilizing a work-integrated learning model. Students can exit early with a Diploma (1 year) or Advanced Diploma (2 years).
